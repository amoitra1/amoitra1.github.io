New York for the Weekend

March 6, 2024

Introduction

I went on a trip to New York City for a weekend to visit a friend! The experience was fantastic, and this post is made to show some interesting pictures in roughly chronological order.

Flushing

This was the first place we visited as soon as I got out of LaGaurdia.

The High Line

With a view of Chelsea's eccentric apartments.

Brookyln

I didn't take many picture of Brooklyn but here is a resturant we ate at. It was One Piece themed!

Guggenheim

A peculiar building.

Nike Store

We found ourselves in this place for an unusually long time. It was a strikingly designed five-story store, and I just couldn't resist putting this photo of LeBron here.

St. Patrick's Cathedral

We stumbled upon this place unexpectedly, and I didn't expect it to be this big. It had a eerie atmosphere that strangely captivated me. I think it was one of the best highlights of my trip.

The Flatiron

On our way to SoHo, it started snowing and it was so beautiful.

Lego Store

This is me at the Lego Store! It was another incredibly well-designed store where anyone could create their own Lego masterpieces. I also really liked the Nintendo store nearby.

Times Square

The snow was the best part.

Long Island City

Another amazing view!

Metropolitan Museum of Art

We spent more than 5 hours and still could not finish the entire musuem.

Skyscape

Another extraordinary experience! While I'm not usually drawn to anything spy-related, this place completely fascinated me.

Views

This view of Manhatten is from Queens. Overall, I had a really fun time in New York!
